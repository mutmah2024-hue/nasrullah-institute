FAQ - Nasrullah Institute

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about enrolling at Nasrullah Institute

1. How do I enroll at Nasrullah Institute?
You can enroll by clicking the "Enroll Now" button on the homepage or the courses page. This will take you to the enrollment form where you fill in your details and submit.
2. What courses do you offer?
We offer comprehensive Quran memorization programs, Tajweed courses, Qira'at studies, and Arabic language studies for students of age 10 and above.
3. Are there online classes?
Currently, we focus on in-person classes. We are exploring online options, so please check back soon for updates on online learning opportunities.
4. What is the fee structure?
Fees vary depending on the course and duration. For detailed fee information, please contact our admissions office through the contact page or email info@nasrullahinstitute.com.
5. Do you provide certificates?
Yes! Upon successful completion of courses, students receive certificates recognized both locally and internationally.
